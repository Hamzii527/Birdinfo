ALL BIRDS INFORMATION

Birds are warm-blooded animals with feathers, wings, and beaks. They are found all over the world and come in many shapes, sizes, and colors. Most birds can fly, although some, like ostriches and penguins, cannot.

European Robin

The European robin is a small, friendly bird commonly found across Europe. It has a distinctive orange-red breast, brown back, and a sweet, melodic song. Often seen in gardens and woodlands, the robin is known for its curious nature and is not shy around humans.

Black Guillemot

The Black Guillemot is a striking seabird found along rocky coasts and islands in the North Atlantic. It has black plumage with a bold white wing patch during the breeding season, and bright red legs and feet that stand out against its dark body.
